Driving Directions:

From I-95 North
  1. From Interstate 95, take Exit 99A onto Interstate 16 East.
  2. Proceed approximately 2.3 miles (3.7 km) on I-16 to Exit 160, Highway 307 Dean Forest Road.
  3. At the top of the ramp, at the light, on Exit 160, turn left onto Highway 307 North.
  4. Proceed approximately 6 miles (9.7 km) on 307 until it ends at Highway 25, turn right on Highway 25 and proceed approximately 1/2 mile, the GPA Annex 1 Building will be on the left.
  5. End at 82 Main Street.
