Driving Directions:

From I-95
  1. From Interstate 95, take Exit 29.
  2. Take a right onto Highway 17 South.
  3. Continue straight through the red light. This is the beginning of Highway 82 West.
  4. Continue on Highway 82 West for approximately 58 miles (93.3km) to Waycross, GA.
  5. In Waycross, take Highway 84 West for approximately 149 miles (239.8km) to Bainbridge.
  6. In Bainbridge, take the Highway 84 West bypass around Bainbridge. After approximately 5 miles (8km), take the exit for Spring Creek Road.
  7. At the end of the ramp, make a left at the red light onto Spring Creek Road.
  8. In approximately 2 miles (3.2km), the Bainbridge State Docks will be on the left.
