Where you do business can be just as important as what you do. Selection of the best site depends on many factors, including incentives, work force, utilities, and transportation options. With Georgia’s Commercial Corridors tool, finding the best location is now easier than ever. County information, photos, and building and property site specs are regularly updated to ensure you have the right information to make the right decision. (Requires Adobe Flash)

Instructions

  1. BEGIN Once the Terms & Conditions are agreed to, click on an available corridor in the menu to the right of the Georgia map. An enlarged map of that corridor will appear.
  2. Next, click on any county, for example Bibb, and a summary of general information about the county will be displayed, including total population and distribution centers. Click on the “Incentives,” “Work Force,” or “Contact” tab for county-specific statistics and information.
  3. Next, the “Building” or Property” tab in the upper-right corner, and a menu of buildings or properties will be displayed. Click on any building or property in the menu, for example the Interstate 75 Business Park property, and general information specific to that property will appear such as size, location and environment. An applicable photo or aerial view, if available, will also be displayed. Click on the “Utility” tab to display water, sewer, gas and electric specifics, the “Transportation” tab for highway information, intermodal and airport access, as well as distance to Georgia's deepwater terminals, and the “Contact” tab for Georgia Ports Authority and county contacts, including email and phone and fax numbers.
  4. Next, select another county, click “Home” in the lower left or “Back” in the right corner above the photo to return to the Georgia map and menu of available corridors.
